From On Premise Infrastructure to Cloud optimized Systems Architecture which can help you bring in cost savings and agility to business processes.

### Benefits of Migrating to the cloud are.
Lower Operational Costs
Moving your legacy business process to cloud reduces operational costs by helping you update, modernize your business logic and lowering maintenance costs by utilizing benefits of cloud based solutions and its support.

### Business Agility
Legacy business logic need your staff to be static and slow i.e. Stick to Office hours, Non-Flexible work conditions due to On-Prem Access restrictions etc.
With Cloud comes Centralized Authenticated Access to your whole infrastructure within your realm, it allows for remote work, agility by design and cost benefits due to speed of delivery that’s possible.

### Faster and Flexible Organization
Legacy business logic need your staff to at the location and it offers no opportunity to leverage workforce i.e. Stick to Office hours, Non-Flexible work conditions due to On-Prem Access restrictions etc.
With Cloud comes Centralized Authenticated Access to your whole infrastructure within your realm, it allows for remote work, agility by design and cost benefits due to speed of delivery that’s possible.
