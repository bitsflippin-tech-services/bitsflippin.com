# Master the Modern Tech Stack
At **bitsflippin**, we don't just teach theory. Our training programs are designed by active consultants who build and maintain the systems we teach. Whether you are an individual engineer or a corporate team, we bridge the gap between "knowing" and "deploying."

## Our Training Tracks

### 1. Cloud & DevOps Engineering
Learn to orchestrate global infrastructure with a focus on automation.
* **Infrastructure as Code:** Mastering Terraform and OpenTofu.
* **CI/CD Mastery:** Setting up production-grade pipelines in **GitHub Actions, DroneCI, and Jenkins**.
* **Cloud Multi-Tenancy:** Deployment strategies for **AWS, Hetzner, and Digital Ocean**.

### 2. Cloud Security & Networking
Hardening the flip of every bit.
* **Zero Trust Architecture:** Implementing secure tunnels and private VPCs.
* **Container Security:** Best practices for **Podman** and **Kubernetes** runtime security.
* **Network Defense:** Advanced configurations for networking hardware and firewalls.

### 3. OpenSource Software & Linux
Empowering your team with the freedom of OpenSource.
* **Linux Administration:** Deep dives into Debian/Ubuntu and RHEL-based systems.
* **Database Management:** Optimization and scaling for **MariaDB and PostgreSQL**.
* **Self-Hosting:** Transitioning from SaaS to self-hosted IaaS to reduce overhead.
