### Developing good web application is not easy but we are here to assist you.

### We use the following stack for applications:
- PostgreSQL or MongoDB / MariaDB
- Express
- NodeJS
- React with UI Toolkits
-  We also support other framework and languages based on client needs.
- We follow industries best practicies to deliver great code quality, and high performant applications.
-  CI/CD processes to automatically check, integrate and deploy new updates.
-  We use Linux servers to host the applications and maintain the fleets with up to date patches and fixes.
-  We are focused on quality rather than quantity hence pick our projects carefully and strive to maintain positive client experience.
